The Conversion Suite

Discovery is not enough. Conversion is the product.

Every business on NexaNG gets the tools to turn a profile visit into money. No third-party redirects. No broken links. Everything closes on platform.

Booking System
Calendar scheduling, time slot selection, SMS reminders via Termii, and optional Paystack deposit. Customers book without leaving the page.
NexaShop
Full product catalogue, cart, Paystack checkout, digital delivery, inventory tracking, and discount codes. A storefront inside every profile.
NexaChat
On-platform messaging, auto-responder, lead capture on every conversation, and a full chat history. No WhatsApp dependency required.
WhatsApp Connect
Pre-filled WhatsApp CTA on every profile. Pro tier: broadcast list builder to send promos to opted-in customers.
Lead Capture Forms
'Request a Quote' and 'Get a Callback' forms on every profile. Validated leads delivered via dashboard and email. PPL billing for high-ticket verticals.
Niche Articles
Sellers write keyword-rich articles that appear in their niche feed. Builds SEO authority and positions them as the expert buyers trust.
NexaVerified Premium Service

When trust isn't enough — we become the service.

NexaNG employs, vets, dispatches, and guarantees its own field technicians. A first-party service layer that transforms NexaNG from a marketplace into infrastructure.

Background Verified
Every Nexa technician passes a police clearance check and identity verification via VerifyMe Nigeria before their first job.
Skills Certified
Techs complete a NexaNG assessment and practical skills test for their specific trade category. Rated and ranked.
Service Guaranteed
Satisfaction or we send another technician — or refund. Payment held in Paystack escrow until customer confirms completion.
Real-Time Tracking
Watch your technician travel to you live on a map. GPS-tracked from dispatch to arrival to job completion.
Nexa Ops Support
Direct channel to our operations team before, during, and after the service. 2-hour SLA on all disputes.
Secure Escrow
Pay via Paystack. Funds held securely — released only when the customer confirms the job is done to standard.
